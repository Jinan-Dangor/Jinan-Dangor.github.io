About Me

Hi! I'm Andrew Trout, though you'll usually see me using my handle Jinan Dangor (pronounced however you'd like to pronounce it). I'm an amateur puzzle and game designer born in Newcastle, Australia, and currently residing in Sydney. I regularly run puzzle events and TRPGs, and spends a great deal of my spare time on a variety of side projects.

Here you can find all of those projects that've reached a stage that makes them remotely acceptable to display publicly.

If you'd like to get in contact, hit me up at jinandangor@gmail.com.

I also regularly attend a club run by writer and puzzle maker Sarina H. Find her work over at her website, Scarlett Herring (original writing and mystery games).
