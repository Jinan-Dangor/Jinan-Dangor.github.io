Tabletop RolePlaying Games

I love TRPGs! I'm one of the perma-DMs who's run more games than they've played. In the process I've made my fair share of campaigns and encounters as well as a bunch of my own systems.

A lot of my work doesn't end up being worth posting online (sometimes my work is half in my head and half in a spreadsheet, and the former half requires some work before I can post it online). That said, who knows when I'll be struck with the inspiration to write up a successful adventure? Watch this space!
