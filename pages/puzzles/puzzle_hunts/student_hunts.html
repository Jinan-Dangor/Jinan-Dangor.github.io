Spanning an entire year, the student-themed puzzle hunts were themed after helping some students through problems they were having at uni. Tragically, the meta-hunt, The Final Exam, never got played due to the real life final exams of myself and my friends.

The hunts were as follows:
  • The MedSci Student
  • The Engineering Student
  • The AFTRS Student
  • The Business Student
  • The Final Exam
The student races each involved 6 puzzles and a meta-puzzle. The Final Exam involves 4 puzzles, each of which utilises one of the previous four hunts in a novel way.

Any puzzles no longer solvable due to physical exhibits or otherwise have been signposted.
