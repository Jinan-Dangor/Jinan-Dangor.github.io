Outer Wilds is one of my favourite games of all time, I couldn't resist making a hunt for it. If you haven't played Outer Wilds (you should) this hunt's lore and the theme of the puzzles will make less sense. That said, it is also spoiler-light, so if you solve this hunt before playing the game it shouldn't spoil much for you.

It involves 6 puzzles and a meta-puzzle, and can be found here.
