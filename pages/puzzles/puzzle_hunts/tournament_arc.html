Tournament Arc was my second puzzle hunt, and with the help of some friends was significantly larger than the first. You can find it here.

It contains 18 puzzles and a metapuzzle.

Unfortunately, several of the puzzles are now unsolvable thanks to relying on knowledge specific to my college.
The following features suffer from this problem:
  • Coffee Break - The campus Coffee Cart served all the coffees this puzzle references. It should still be solvable, just more difficult (I'm relatively sure most of these coffees are standard).
  • The Best Friend - This puzzle references group themes, as hidden in each group was a letter attached to some object or piece of furniture.
